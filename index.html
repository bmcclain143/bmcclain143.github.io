Shipwaves Overview

Shipwaves is a full stack ocean logistics platform. Shipwaves provides India's first ocean freight rate search engine for shippers to search and book open freight to thousands of port pairs, both to and from India, and a dashboard to manage their shipments. The company aims to make global logistics more reliable, convenient, and provide shippers with visibility into lowest cost and fastest options available without the hassle and frustration that has previously been experienced in this sector.
