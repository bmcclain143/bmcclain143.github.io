Deal Positives

The solution that Shipwaves offers is a user-friendly interface that maintains a human component as to introduce gradual transformation within this industry.

There are various facets of the Indian logistics industry that increase cost - heavy paperwork, slow adoption of technology, and multiple coordination agencies to name a few. Shipwaves has the potential to reduce these types of costs through their solution, reviving the ocean freight industry and increasing profits.

Millions of SMBs do not have the ability to go global because they lack understanding around the process in order to do so due to numerous agents and multi-level freight forwarding companies. Shipwaves could be the solution to this problem, creating a compelling growth opportunity for both the SMBs involved and the ocean freight industry generally.
