Shipwaves Overview

Shipwaves is a full stack ocean logistics platform providing the first ocean freight rate marketplace for importers and exporters from India to search and book open freight to thousands of port pairs, and a platform to track their shipments. Shipwaves handles all of the coordination regarding shipping specifics that shippers have historically done on their own as well as provides in house senior logistics experts who make tactical recommendations throughout the shipping process. The company aims to create a more reliable and convenient global logistics environment by allowing shippers to instantly compare rates and book a service that fits their needs.
