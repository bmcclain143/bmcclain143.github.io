Shipwaves Overview

Shipwaves is a full stack ocean logistics platform. Shipwaves provides India's first ocean freight rate search engine for shippers to search and book open freight to thousands of port pairs, both to and from India, and a dashboard to manage their shipments. Use the links above to learn more.
